NFC Smart Tags — A New Service Layer

Inexpensive NFC chips ($0.25–$1.00 each) paired with simple smartphone apps create powerful tracking, verification, and automation systems your clients didn't know they needed.

Dog Kennel / Pet Services

Kennel Run Tags — Care Logging

NFC tag on each kennel door. Staff tap to log feedings, meds, play sessions, and wellness checks. Creates an auditable care record and auto-generates daily owner updates.

ROI: Eliminates handwritten care logs, cuts "How's my dog?" calls by ~70%, builds trust with documented proof of care.
Dog Kennel / Pet Services

Collar Tags — Pet Identity & Check-In

NFC-enabled collar tag for each boarder. Tap to instantly pull up the pet's full profile — feeding instructions, meds, behavior notes, emergency vet — without searching a computer.

ROI: New staff can care for any dog correctly on day one. Zero mix-ups on feeding or medication.
CPA / Accounting

Asset Tags — Client Equipment Tracking

For CPA firms that manage asset depreciation schedules: NFC tags on client equipment (vehicles, machinery, office assets). Tap during site visits to verify the asset exists, log its condition, and update the depreciation record.

ROI: Physical asset verification for audits. Eliminates manual inventory counts. Photo + GPS + timestamp in one tap.
CPA / Accounting

Secure Document Handoff Tags

NFC tag on client file folders or document envelopes. When a client drops off tax documents, staff taps the folder to log receipt — creating a timestamped chain of custody. Client gets an auto-confirmation text.

ROI: No more "Did you get my documents?" calls. Auditable chain of custody for sensitive tax records.
Insurance Agency

NFC Business Cards — Instant Quote Requests

Hand a prospect an NFC-enabled business card at a networking event. They tap it to their phone and land on a personalized quote request form pre-filled with the agent's info and a tracking code.

ROI: Converts networking into measurable leads. No forgotten business cards — the link lives on their phone.
Insurance Agency

Property Inspection Tags

For commercial property inspections: NFC tag at each inspection point (HVAC unit, electrical panel, roof access). Inspector taps to log condition, take a photo, and timestamp the visit — all feeding directly into the policy file.

ROI: Faster inspections, standardized documentation, GPS-verified proof the inspector was on-site.
Local Service Companies

Job Site Tags — Crew Time & Invoice Trigger

NFC tag at each client location. Crew taps on arrival and departure. The system logs job duration, calculates labor costs, and auto-sends the invoice the moment the job is marked complete.

ROI: Eliminates paper timesheets. Invoices go out same-day. GPS proof of service for disputes.
Local Service Companies

Equipment Maintenance Tags

NFC tag on each piece of equipment (pressure washer, ladder, van). Tap to log usage, report damage, or confirm a maintenance check. AI tracks usage patterns and alerts you before something breaks.

ROI: Prevents costly breakdowns. Tracks equipment across crews. Simplifies fleet/tool management.
